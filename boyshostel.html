Admission

The applications for accommodation in the Hostel are generally much more than the number of seats available. The selections are made entirely on the basis of merit and need.

Admission Procedure

It is a simple procedure of filling forms and paying fees.It can be done in much short time after the merit. (Note: Student's hostel admission will be proceed on the basis of merit list.)

Lodging

The rooms in the Hostel are three seaters (three students in a room), two seaters (two students in a room) and two seaters (six students in a room) only.

Mess

The Mess of the Hostel is run entirely under the supervision of the Rector and Principle. Mess for hostler’s is mandatory.
